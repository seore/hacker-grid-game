How to Play

Your goal is to create a continuous circuit from the SOURCE (green) to the TARGET (pink).

  • Click on a tile to rotate it 90° clockwise.
  • You cannot rotate the source, target, or faded empty tiles.
  • When a valid path exists, the connected tiles will glow and access is granted.

Try to solve each level in as few moves as possible — beat or match the Par shown in the HUD.

How to Play

  • Green = source node. Pink = target node. They never move.
  • Cyan tiles are the only ones you can rotate.
  • Click a cyan tile to rotate it 90°. Align bars to form one clean path from green to pink.
  • Beat the timer. Fewer moves = cleaner hack.

Tip: watch the neon flow when you solve a pattern – that’s the data stream you just hijacked.
